Calisthenics



L sit:

1. put to arm on the floor
2.open your leg straight till there in the floor

elbow lever:

1. Put your elbow on your stomach
2. lean a little bit infront
3. open your leg straight for them to be in the air

Handstand:

1. Train your balense
2. put your hands on the floor
3. make your body straight

Frog stand:

1. put your hand on the floor
2. put your knee on your lower tricpes
3. lean infront a little
4. close your leg to be in the air

One arm elbow lever:

1. start with a elbow lever
2. lean your body to the right while putting your left hand in the air for balense

Planche:

1. start with a plank form with open arm while putting your arm oustside
2. lean to the front till your leg are in the air

Muscle Up:

1. know how to do pull
2. hang on the bar while moving front to back
3. when you start moving backwards push yourself on top of the bar to get with straight hand

Human Flag

1. put your hand on the bar without opening them much
2. pull your body to the air to make a straight line
